# The Face in Her Sketchbook

On a Tokyo train, a shy artist drops her sketchbook and a stranger discovers she has drawn his face before they have ever met.

**Title card (segment 1):** NEXT STOP: FIRST LOOK

## Narration script (~59 words, TTS via edge-tts)

> Mina sketches strangers because talking feels impossible. Tonight, she wants one real face before her stop. Then the train lurches. Her sketchbook flies open, pages spinning like white birds, and one portrait lands in a stranger's hands. It is him. Impossible? The lights blush pink. The tunnel blooms around them. For once, Mina looks up before the moment passes.

## Opening hook (first 3-5s of segment 1)

> At 0s, @mina-sketchbook bursts from Mina's hands in extreme close-up as the train jolts; graphite pages whip open mid-air, loose sketches hover for one impossible second, and one portrait sheet snaps flat against the chest of @ren-stranger, revealing his exact face in charcoal.

- **Era**: contemporary 2020s romantic magical realism
- **Lens**: 35mm anamorphic for movement, 50mm shallow depth of field on faces and hands

- **Motion**: fast intimate handheld with snap push-ins, whip-pans between hands and faces, brief slow-motion only for impossible suspended pages

- **Music**: romantic city-pop piano + brushed drums + warm synth pulse, 118 bpm, tender rising 4-note motif in A major that opens wider in segment 2
- **Ambient**: continuous Tokyo train rumble, soft rail clicks, muted station chimes, distant passenger movement, low tunnel wind
- **SFX motif**: the dry flutter of sketchbook pages, recurring whenever the connection between Mina and Ren shifts
- **Mix**: music energetic but intimate, page flutter and train lurches crisp in foreground, ambient rail bed continuous across the stitch

## Story beats

1. Mina's sketchbook bursts open during a sudden train lurch, Ren catches the portrait page, and realizes the face drawn there is his own.
2. The train glides into a tunnel of cherry-blossom light, the impossible portrait comes alive for one breath, and Mina and Ren finally look up at each other.
