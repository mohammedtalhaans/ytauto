# Twelve Lights in the Blackout

During a monsoon blackout in 1995 Mumbai, two strangers on adjoining rooftops find each other through candlelight before the city wakes again.

**Title card (segment 1):** MUMBAI BLACKOUT • 1995

## Narration script (~69 words, TTS via edge-tts)

> Mumbai, 1995. Aanya climbs to the roof to protect twelve tiny flames from the monsoon. Across the wall, Vikram is doing the same. Then the whole city goes dark. No phones. No words. Just rain, breath, and two stubborn pools of light. One lantern lifts. One hand answers. How can a stranger feel familiar? When the streetlights return, the distance is gone. Some love stories begin before anyone speaks.

## Opening hook (first 3-5s of segment 1)

> Extreme close-up at 0s: Aanya's match touches the twelfth clay diya, and the instant the flame blooms, the entire Mumbai skyline behind it shuts off in a rolling blackout wave, tower by tower, reflected upside-down in trembling rainwater around the flame.

- **Era**: 1990s Mumbai monsoon romance with intimate indie period realism and restrained magical realism
- **Lens**: 32mm Super 16mm-style spherical lens, shallow depth of field on hands and faces, slightly deeper focus for rooftop-to-skyline reveals
- **Grade**: warm tungsten-amber against teal monsoon sky, wet-stone highlights, lifted blacks during blackout, soft golden halation around flames
- **Stock**: soft Super 16mm grain emulation with visible rain texture, gentle gate weave, bloom on candles and sodium-vapor streetlights

## Audio bible

- **Music**: sparse Indian classical romance, 78 bpm, harmonium drone and light tabla in Raag Yaman mood, with a single Hindustani slide-flute motif swelling when their eyes meet
- **Ambient**: continuous monsoon rain on concrete, distant thunder, muffled city traffic fading during blackout, occasional rooftop water drips and far electrical hum
- **SFX motif**: the soft flare of match and wick ignition, recurring each time a flame becomes a signal
- **Mix**: music restrained and intimate, rain always present, flame and lantern sounds close and tactile, city hum swelling only as power returns

## Story beats

1. Aanya lights the twelfth diya as the city blackout cascades behind her, then sees Vikram's candle cluster alive across the rain-wet wall.
2. Aanya and Vikram trade silent lantern gestures, Vikram crosses onto her rooftop, and the returning city lights reveal them standing together.
