# The Rain Remembers

A rooftop thief steals a luminous museum relic, only to discover it is a memorial capsule for the person she never stopped grieving.

**Title card (segment 1):** THE RAIN REMEMBERS

## Narration script (~62 words, TTS via edge-tts)

> Mara Vale steals from museums because memories pay better than gold. Tonight, the prize is a glowing relic locked under the city. She cracks the case. The vault wakes. Guards flood in, and rain becomes her only exit. Then the relic opens on the roof. It is not treasure. It is Eli's goodbye. She stole the one thing she should have protected.

## Opening hook (first 3-5s of segment 1)

> At 0s, the artifact's glass case bursts upward into hundreds of frozen crystal shards, each shard reflecting @mara-vale's masked eyes as her gloved hand snatches the glowing memorial prism.

## Style bible

- **Era**: contemporary 2020s urban neo-noir heist thriller with magical-realist memorial technology
- **Lens**: 28mm anamorphic for kinetic vertical action, shallow depth of field on faces and artifact close-ups, deeper focus during chase beats
- **Grade**: cool cyan shadows with warm gold artifact highlights, crushed blacks, glossy rain reflections, soft highlight bloom
- **Stock**: digital cinema with high-contrast night grain, subtle halation around lasers and lightning
- **Motion**: fast handheld pursuit camera, whip-pans between action beats, snap zooms on the artifact and emotional reveal

## Audio bible

- **Music**: urgent hybrid trap strings + pulsing piano, 128 bpm, tense minor-key motif in E minor that breaks into mournful cello in segment 2
- **Ambient**: continuous heavy rooftop rain, distant traffic hiss, muffled museum alarms, thunder rolling under the score
- **SFX motif**: the memorial-prism emits a soft glass heartbeat chime whenever it pulses or reveals a memory
- **Mix**: music forward and driving, rain constant but ducked under narration, artifact chime crisp and emotionally foregrounded

## Story beats

1. Mara steals the glowing memorial prism from the museum vault, triggers the security system, and escapes upward through shattered glass toward the storm.
2. On the rainy rooftop, Mara opens the stolen prism and realizes it preserves Eli's final memory, forcing her to stop running and grieve.

## Planned spectacle / VFX moments

- Segment 1: the glass display case explodes upward in slow motion while red security lasers bend around the glowing prism like liquid wire.
- Segment 1: as Mara sprints through @museum-vault, the artifact throws blue-gold light pulses that briefly freeze guards and raindrops visible through shattered skylights.
- Segment 2: on @rainy-rooftop, the prism levitates from Mara's palm and unfolds into concentric rings of rainwater suspended in mid-air.
- Segment 2: Eli's memorial appears as a human-sized silhouette made of warm gold particles, rain passing through him before the particles collapse back into the artifact.

Night lighting from cold white LEDs and emergency red strobes; palette of graphite, crimson, and icy blue.
- **rainy-rooftop** — rain-lashed museum rooftop with wet tar paper, rusted ventilation fans, glass skylight seams, lightning rods, low parapet, and distant high-rise windows beyond. 1:20 AM storm light from blue lightning, amber city glow, and the artifact; palette of black slate, electric blue, and warm gold.

- **mara-mask** — matte-black half-mask with smoked glass eye lenses, thin silver breathing vents, rain-beaded finish, close-fitting angular shape; worn during the theft, then pulled down for the rooftop reveal.
