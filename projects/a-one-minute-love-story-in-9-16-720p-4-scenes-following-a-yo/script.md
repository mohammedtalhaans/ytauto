# The Silver Thread

A quiet one-minute love story about two strangers whose repeated crossings around a lost silver locket slowly reveal they have been orbiting the same memory for years.

## Style bible

- **Era**: contemporary 2020s indie cinema with intimate urban romantic realism
- **Lens**: 35mm anamorphic for most shots, 50mm close-ups for faces and the locket, shallow depth of field on emotional beats with softly readable vertical backgrounds
- **Grade**: warm amber-teal grade, gentle peach skin tones, slightly lifted blacks, soft highlight roll-off, restrained saturation
- **Stock**: digital cinema with subtle Super 16 inspired grain emulation, mild halation around practical lights, natural texture without heavy stylization
- **Motion**: handheld realism with quiet breathing movement, sparing slow dolly-ins during recognition moments, no fast cuts or flashy transitions

## Audio bible

- **Music**: solo felt piano plus warm chamber strings, 68 bpm, lyrical romantic theme in C major with a recurring four-note rising motif that grows fuller each segment
- **Ambient**: continuous soft city hum, distant traffic, occasional train rumble, rain texture where appropriate, and faint room tone tying every location together
- **SFX motif**: the soft metallic click of the silver locket clasp, recurring each time the locket changes hands or opens
- **Mix**: music remains underscored and continuous in mood, ambient stays subtle, close foreground sounds like breath, footsteps, rain, and the locket click remain clear

## Story beats

1. Segment 1 beat - Maya drops the silver locket on a subway platform, and Leo picks it up just as she disappears behind closing train doors.
2. Segment 2 beat - Leo nearly returns the locket in a bookshop-cafe, but a shy missed moment turns the object from lost property into a mystery between them.
3. Segment 3 beat - In the rain outside the cinema, Maya finally sees Leo holding the locket, and the photograph inside reveals a shared childhood place.
4. Segment 4 beat - They meet at the rooftop garden from the photograph, the locket returns to Maya, and the repeated crossings resolve into a first honest connection.

## Characters

- **leo** — 27-year-old Korean American man, light olive skin with warm undertones, dark brown eyes, black medium-length wavy hair tucked loosely behind one ear, clean-shaven, wearing a faded forest-green chore jacket over a cream waffle-knit shirt, straight black denim trousers, and worn dark brown leather boots; thin round tortoiseshell glasses, faint scar through his left eyebrow, slightly careful posture.
- **maya** — 26-year-old Mexican British woman, medium tan skin with golden undertones, hazel-green eyes, chestnut brown shoulder-length curly hair worn half-pinned with loose strands around her face, wearing a rust-red wool coat over a soft ivory blouse, high-waisted dark indigo jeans, black ankle boots with a low heel, and a narrow tan leather satchel; tiny crescent tattoo behind her right ear, expressive upright posture.

## Settings

- **subway-platform** — underground city subway platform with late-modern tiled walls, brushed steel benches, yellow safety line, scratched timetable boards, and damp concrete floor. Early evening fluorescent strips mix with warm train-window light, creating greenish amber shadows. Dominant palette: oxidized teal, sodium amber, and charcoal grey.
- **bookshop-cafe** — narrow independent bookshop-cafe in a converted brick storefront, with tall wooden shelves, marble-topped counter, brass reading lamps, mismatched bistro tables, and condensation on the front window. Blue-hour light filters through glass while tungsten bulbs glow warmly. Dominant palette: honey wood, brick red, and deep green.
- **rainy-cinema-street** — rain-slick sidewalk outside a small art-house cinema with 1930s facade, glowing marquee bulbs, poster cases, puddled asphalt, and passing taxi reflections. Nighttime light comes from neon signage, streetlamps, and cinema doors. Dominant palette: wet black, warm gold, and muted rose.
- **rooftop-garden** — modest apartment rooftop garden with waist-high brick parapet, weathered wooden planters, string lights, metal folding chairs, and a faded mural wall. Dawn light rises behind nearby buildings, softened by cool haze and warm bulbs. Dominant palette: pale peach, sage green, and soft concrete grey.

## Props

- **silver-locket** — small oval sterling silver locket on a thin matching chain, slightly tarnished around the hinge, engraved with a tiny cursive letter 'M' and a hairline scratch across the back; opens to reveal a faded black-and-white rooftop photograph, usually held in a palm or dangling from fingertips.
